FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? Here are the answers to the most common things our customers ask.

How long does a standard car service take?
A standard interim service usually takes around 1–1.5 hours, while a full service typically takes 2–3 hours. We'll always give you an estimated time when you drop your car off. If there's anything unexpected, we'll call you immediately.
Do you work on all makes and models?
Yes, we work on all makes and models — from small hatchbacks to large SUVs and vans. Whether it's a Ford, Volkswagen, BMW, Toyota, Vauxhall or anything else, our mechanics have the tools and expertise to handle it.
Will servicing my car with you void my manufacturer's warranty?
Absolutely not. Under UK law (Block Exemption Regulations), you are free to have your vehicle serviced by any qualified independent garage without affecting your manufacturer warranty, as long as manufacturer-recommended parts and procedures are followed — which we always do.
How much does a car service cost?
Pricing depends on the type of service and your specific vehicle. We always provide a clear, upfront quote before any work begins. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges — what we quote is what you pay. Contact us or use our booking form to get a personalised estimate.
Do you offer same-day appointments?
We do offer same-day appointments where available, especially for diagnostics and urgent repairs. We recommend calling us directly on 0116 200 1234 for the quickest response if you need an urgent slot.
Do you provide a warranty on repairs?
Yes. All our repairs and parts come with a warranty. If anything we've worked on gives you trouble, bring it back and we'll put it right. We stand behind every job we do.
